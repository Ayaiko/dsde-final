# Does air quality affect reporting in the Traffy Fondue app?
## Traffy Fondue 
is a platform that allows citizens to report urban problems to local authorities, such as damaged roads, garbage, flooding, or environmental issues.
### When air quality becomes poor 
(for example, during high PM2.5 pollution), people are more likely to submit reports related to environmental problems through the app. These reports may include:
- Open burning (trash or agricultural burning)
- Vehicles emitting black smoke
- Dust pollution sources
- Other environmental hazards affecting air quality
Therefore, poor air quality can increase the number of environmental reports in the Traffy Fondue system, helping authorities identify pollution sources and take corrective actions.
### Then
Therefore, we incorporated external air quality data and analyzed it together with the reports collected from the Traffy Fondue app to examine the relationship between air quality and reporting behavior. By combining these two datasets, we aim to evaluate whether changes in air pollution levels influence the number or type of reports submitted by users. This analysis helps us better understand how environmental conditions may affect public reporting patterns and provides insights into how urban environmental issues are reflected through the app’s reporting system.
